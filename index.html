&Attend - Past work
He is the creator of the online learning platform &Attend that gives learners from disadvantaged background access to high quality education in South Africa.
Challenge: When designing the interface, Sbonakaliso employed the mobile-first approach. The website looked and felt amazing on mobile phones and small tablets but on larger tablets and personal computers, the buttons were oversized and spaned the entire screen. So he researched other methods that simplify responsive design. He found an excellent book by UXPin recommending the use of a card based interface, this was an appropriate design for the timetable page of the platform.


Skibha
A website for an artist who creates custom art from personal photographs.
Challenge: Sbonakaliso faced the problem of having too much say on a finite web page, so he turned the problem on it's head and asked himself: "What if I don't tell them how great Luyanda's art is? What if I show them?". He then adopted a minimalistic approach that focused the user's attention the artwork, and not the interface.
