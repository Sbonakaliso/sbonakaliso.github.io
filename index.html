Virtual Tutorial Rooms on the Web.

Welcome to The Tutorial Centre

The Tutorial Centre offers virtual tutorial rooms on the web, which makes tuition convenient for both the student and the tutor.

Functions

The virtual tutorial rooms are private sessions between a tutor and one or more students. Student-Tutor pairs/groups can use the service by having one member of the group share her URL with everyone. Once everyone has clicked the URL then, they can all edit (simultaneously) the page, chat (internal IM) and speak (voice chat).

Payments

The agreed upon amount will be payed to The Tutorial Centre's account and after deduction of the mandatory commission it will be transferred to the tutor's account.
